## 2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data that is necessary to run an ecommerce store, process orders, provide customer service, and meet legal obligations.

We may collect:

- Account data: name, email address, password authentication details, account ID, and login records.
- Checkout and order data: name, phone number, email address, delivery address, city, delivery area, order notes, items ordered, product size, color, quantity, order value, delivery fee, order status, and payment status.
- Payment data: payment reference, payment provider, payment status, transaction metadata, and fraud-prevention information. We do not intentionally store full card numbers on our website.
- Customer support data: messages, complaints, return or exchange requests, delivery questions, and related communications.
- Marketing data: newsletter or promotional preferences, consent records, and interactions with promotions, where enabled.
- Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, timestamps, session data, cookies, and similar analytics or security logs.
- Admin and operational data: administrator accounts, order notification email settings, and internal records needed to manage orders and delivery.

We do not intentionally collect special category data, such as health information, political opinions, religious beliefs, biometric data, or similar sensitive information. Please do not submit sensitive information through order notes or support messages.

## 3. How We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data directly from you when you:

- Create an account or sign in.
- Add items to cart and place an order.
- Select a delivery area.
- Complete payment through Paystack.
- Contact us by email, phone, form, or social media.
- Subscribe to marketing, promotions, or sales notifications.
- Browse the website, where cookies or similar technologies are used.

We may also receive payment confirmation or transaction status data from Paystack and technical service data from our hosting, database, email, and infrastructure providers.

## 4. Why We Use Personal Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

- To create and manage customer accounts.
- To process carts, checkout, orders, payments, delivery, returns, exchanges, and cancellations.
- To verify payment and prevent fraud.
- To send order confirmations, shipping updates, cancellation notices, and customer service messages.
- To manage delivery areas, fees, and order fulfilment.
- To respond to enquiries, complaints, and support requests.
- To maintain website security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.
- To improve products, user experience, inventory planning, promotions, and website performance.
- To send marketing communications, where you have opted in or where the law permits us to do so.
- To comply with accounting, tax, audit, consumer protection, payment, data protection, and other legal obligations.
- To establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

Where Ghana's Data Protection Act applies, we process personal data in line with the principles of accountability, lawfulness, purpose specification, compatibility, data quality, openness, security safeguards, and data subject participation.

Where GDPR applies, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

- Contract: to process orders, payments, delivery, customer accounts, returns, exchanges, and support.
- Legal obligation: to keep records required for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, consumer protection, and regulatory compliance.
- Legitimate interests: to operate and secure the website, prevent fraud, manage inventory, improve services, respond to customer enquiries, and conduct limited business analytics.
- Consent: for optional marketing, certain cookies, and any processing where consent is legally required.
- Vital interests or public interest: only where strictly necessary in exceptional circumstances.

If we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect processing that happened before consent was withdrawn.

We share personal data only where necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

We may share personal data with:

- Supabase, which supports authentication, database, storage, and backend services.

Where GDPR applies, and personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we will rely on an appropriate lawful transfer mechanism, such as adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, contractual safeguards, or another permitted mechanism.

## 9. How Long We Keep Personal Data

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

Typical retention periods are:

- Account data: for as long as the account remains active, then for a reasonable period after closure where needed for security, legal, or dispute purposes.
- Order and payment records: for the period required for accounting, tax, audit, fraud prevention, customer service, and legal compliance.
- Customer support messages: for as long as needed to resolve the issue and maintain business records.
- Marketing preferences: until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent, plus any suppression record needed to respect your opt-out.
- Technical logs: for a limited period needed for security, troubleshooting, and analytics.

We may retain data longer if required by law, a dispute, investigation, audit, or legal claim.

## 10. Security

No website or online transmission is completely secure. Customers should keep account passwords confidential and contact us immediately if they suspect unauthorised account activity.
